## Thai Food

|----------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|

| Tom Yum Goong  | A hot and sour shrimp soup that's both tangy and spicy. | Som Tum        | A refreshing and spicy salad made with shredded green papaya, tomatoes, green beans, peanuts, and chili peppers.
